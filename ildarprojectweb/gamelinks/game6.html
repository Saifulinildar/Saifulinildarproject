Facts

  • Genre: Action RPG
  • Publisher: Gamevil
  • Developer: BLUEHOLE GINNO
  • Global Launch: November 15, 2016
  • Platforms: iOS, Android
  • Stores: App Store, Google Play
  • Price: Free, In-App Purchases
  • Languages: English, Spanish, German, French, Russian, Portuguese, Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian, Korean, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified/Traditional)

Features

  • -Refined action RPG gameplay where players can smoothly transition between human and Devilian form.
  • -Signature mechanic, Devilian Mode, offers different set of powerful skills.
  • -Next-generation graphics using game engine Unity.
  • -Three selectable characters with distinct play styles.
  • -Devilstone system, skill customization and various equipment sets offer character specialization.
  • -Different game modes: Campaign, Raid, Arena, Daily Dungeons and Temple of Inferno.
